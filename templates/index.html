Defending Livestock, Safeguarding Futures: Disease Prevention for Healthy Cattle

Lumpy skin disease (LSD) is a viral disease primarily affecting cattle. It is caused by the lumpy skin disease virus (LSDV), a member of the Capripoxvirus genus. LSD is characterized by fever, nodules on the skin, and general discomfort in affected animals.
